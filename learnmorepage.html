Water Availability in India

Learn more about the current situation and challenges.

Current Situation

India faces significant challenges regarding water availability, with many regions experiencing water scarcity. Rapid urbanization, industrialization, and population growth exacerbate this issue.

Challenges

Some of the key challenges include inadequate infrastructure for water storage and distribution, over-extraction of groundwater, pollution of water sources, and inefficient agricultural practices.

Initiatives

Various initiatives are being undertaken to address these challenges, including the construction of dams and reservoirs, promotion of water conservation practices, implementation of water recycling and purification technologies, and awareness campaigns.
